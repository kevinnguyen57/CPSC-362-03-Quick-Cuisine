About QC

PURPOSE
Quick Cuisine is a web application designed for users to interact with for easy search of recipes. Users that have limited food in their fridge and/or cabinets can sometimes not know what they could possibly make with what they have. Users will be able to input the ingredients that they have on a search bar and receive numerous results of recipes to make with such ingredients. Quick Cuisine will provide detailed information for each recipe for easy use for all users
